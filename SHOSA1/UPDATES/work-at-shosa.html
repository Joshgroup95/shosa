The Beginning of SHOSA Welfare policy on 10th November 2020

12th Nov 2020
Standard high school igorora old students association is an association that brings together old students of the named school and has its headquarters in igorora town council ibanda district.

SHOSA started in April 2020 an initiative that was brought by one of the colleagues a one Twinamatsiko Medard who happen to be one of the Old Students of Standard High school Igorora

The initiative was welcomed by other members that called for the formation of interim Committee that would manage the association After successful meetings and consultations, a 13 member committee was formed headed by Tumwikirize Enock that spearheaded mobilisation, constitution making, registration with the government, account opening among others.

In the Month of July, official recruiting of Members to Join SHOSA began and by the End of August 2020, SHOSA was was fully registered with URSB and ibanda district authorities with a working body and Account Number in Centenary Bank.

SHOSA has since undertaken a wide range of activities including reuniting all old students, planning reunions, and running a welfare policy that incorporates saving and credit scheme, assisting members on times of loosing loved ones and functions.
