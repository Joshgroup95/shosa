SHOSA invites you to join the team and be part of founders of this great association

10th Nov 2020
We the Old Students of Standard High school Igorora here by jointly come together to constitute ourselves into this non partisan association with a strong spirit of togetherness so as to play an active role in the social and economic welfare amongst ourselves, our school and our communities at large. SHOSA is an association whose aims and objectives are stated here under, namely:
SHOSA Objectives
    1. To promote unity and collaboration with a view of enhancing the social relations and welfare among Old students of Standard high school Igorora.

    2. To promote developmental relations among members, for the benefit of the association, school and community.

    3. To promote financial development of the members through empowerment with financial ideas, opportunities, pulling resources and any other means deemed appropriate by the members.

    4. To provide a forum for the exchange of ideas and knowledge related to profession, experience and financial management among the members.

    5. To offer charitable services to the school and its community in areas of social, economic and developmental aspects.

    6. To collaborate with other associations, government, organizations, bodies or authorities whose aims and objectives are geared towards the welfare of the association.

    7. To strengthen the financial capacity of the association through fund raising activities, donations. Contributions, grants, gifts, membership fee, and any other means deemed appropriate by the association so as to enable attainment of the above objectives.

    8. Any other objectives that are consistent with those expressly mentioned and deemed appropriate by the general assembly shall be applicable to the association.

Who can Join;
Ordinary Members,

These shall be the Old students of Standard high school Igorora who have paid Membership and subscription fee and have been successfully approved by the existing executive committee.

Associate members

These shall be individuals or Organizations who have expressed their interest through writing and have been approved by the existing executive committee and have fully paid membership and Subscription fee.

MEMBERSHIP AND SUBSCRIPTION:



Ordinary members shall pay a membership fee of 20,000 Uganda Shillings. And Associate members shall pay a membership fee of 40,000Ug.Shs. All members shall pay a subscription fee of 10,000shs annually to the finance secretary or association's account number and be offered an Official receipt.
