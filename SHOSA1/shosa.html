Shosa Objectives

    1. To promote unity and collaboration with a view of enhancing the social relations and welfare among Old students of Standard high school igorora
    2. To promote developmental relations among members, for the benefit of the association, school and community.
    3. To promote financial development of the members through empowerment with financial ideas, opportunities, pulling resources and any other means deemed appropriate by the members.

About Shosa

The name of the association shall be STANDARD HIGH SCHOOL IGORORA OLD STUDENTS ASSOCIATION (SHOSA) herein referred to as the "association".

The headquarters of the association shall be situated in Igorora. This shall be liable to change by the general assembly.
